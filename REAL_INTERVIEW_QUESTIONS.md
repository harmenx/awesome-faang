# REAL_INTERVIEW_QUESTIONS (FAANG)

This file contains **real, high-frequency behavioral questions** asked at FAANG-level companies, mapped to **what they are actually testing** and **how candidates fail**.

Use this to pressure-test your stories.

---

## 1. Failure & Accountability

### “Tell me about your biggest failure.”
**They are testing:**
- Ownership
- Learning speed
- Ego control

**Strong signal:**
- You caused the failure
- You detected it early
- You changed future behavior

**Weak signal (fail):**
- External blame
- “It wasn’t really my fault”
- Cosmetic failure

---

### “Tell me about a time you made a bad decision.”
**They are testing:**
- Judgment under uncertainty
- Reflection

**Strong signal:**
- Decision framework
- Missing info
- Updated model after outcome

**Fail mode:**
- No framework
- Hand-wavy intuition

---

## 10. Universal Closing Question

**They are testing:**

- Narrative control

**Fail mode:**

---

## Final Rule

Every answer should clearly show:

- **You owned something**
- **You made decisions**
- **You learned**
- **The business moved because of you**

If any of these are missing, the answer is weak.
