# Algorithms Resources for FAANG Prep

A curated list of tutorials, guides, problem sets, and videos to master algorithms for FAANG and top tech interviews.

---

## 1. **Learning Platforms**

- [LeetCode](https://leetcode.com/) – Essential for coding interview prep. Focus on **Top 100 Liked Questions** and **company-specific problem sets**.  
- [HackerRank](https://www.hackerrank.com/domains/algorithms) – Good for practicing algorithm fundamentals.  
- [Codeforces](https://codeforces.com/) – Competitive programming to improve speed and problem-solving.  
- [CodeChef](https://www.codechef.com/practice) – Structured practice by difficulty and topic.

## 5. **Books**

- *Cracking the Coding Interview* by Gayle Laakmann McDowell – Classic FAANG prep book.  
- *Elements of Programming Interviews* (EPI) – Problems organized by topic and difficulty.  
- *Introduction to Algorithms (CLRS)* – Comprehensive reference for deep understanding.  
- *Algorithm Design Manual* by Steven Skiena – Practical approach to problem-solving.  

---

## 6. **Problem Patterns & Strategies**

- [Neetcode Patterns](https://neetcode.io/) – Common coding interview patterns: Two pointers, sliding window, DFS/BFS, binary search, DP.  
- [Blind 75](https://leetcode.com/list/gnqwr3/) – High-yield curated list of 75 problems often asked at FAANG.  
- [LeetCode Explore](https://leetcode.com/explore/) – Structured topic-based challenges for algorithm practice.  

---

## 7. **Practice Challenges**

- [LeetCode Weekly Contest](https://leetcode.com/contest/) – Simulate timed interviews.  
- [Codeforces Div2/Div3](https://codeforces.com/) – For competitive programming practice.  
- [Advent of Code](https://adventofcode.com/) – Fun, algorithmic problem-solving challenges.  
- [Project Euler](https://projecteuler.net/) – Math-heavy algorithm problems.  

---

> Tip: Combine **pattern recognition** + **timed problem-solving** + **revision of mistakes** to maximize interview readiness. Track your progress in a spreadsheet or Notion to identify weak topics.
